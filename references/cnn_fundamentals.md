## Summary
This seminal work introduced LeNet-5, establishing the core principles of Convolutional Neural Networks: local receptive fields, shared weights, and spatial down-sampling (pooling). These concepts are the foundation of all modern computer vision models, including the MCNN15 architecture implemented in this project.
